Concerts & Festivals

It can only get better when two form of art go hand in hand.. Right moments when it comes to live performances is something no one wants to miss. Photos keep them alive! Private concerts & Festivals!

Editing and Final Touches

Eventhough natural most often is beautiness within, I have learned throughout my experience as a photographer that editing brings out elements what eyes miss. I edit all my work by myself and do my best for the final results! All the images will be edited and polished before you receive it!

Lightning Setup

I prefer using natural light for my work. Daytime during all the seasons has given perfect lights for my pictures and sometimes a little flash brings out more details!

Open to new photography experiences

I love to capture images of people with different colours in different seasons but I am always open-minded when it comes to new ideas. So don't hesitate to contact me about any style you have in mind! You can find my contact info down below!
